Xoleil Spa Village

Today’s spa philosophy is moving towards prevention, rather than mere pampering. The focus has shifted from self-indulgence to a natural, spiritual, harmonious and necessary even for a restorative experience. Ancient therapeutic traditions of Asia view the body as capable of healing itself by treating the body, mind and spirit as fundamentally one — balancing the flow of ch’i.

Xoleil Spa Village treatments take you on an exotic journey across the rich heritage of etno-wellness traditions of Indonesia; the birthplace of countless natural beauty remedies, rituals and secrets.
